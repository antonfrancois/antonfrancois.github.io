About Me

I am involved with neurosurgeon on the stydy of growth, shape and location of the very deadly glioblatomas, high grade brain tumors. My job is to provide tools for robust MRI normalization. My aim is map brain region from a fully know healphy template brain on a cancerous acquired one. To do so I will use (and opefully improve) the states of the art methods of diffeomorphic image registration such as LDDMM or methamorphosis.
